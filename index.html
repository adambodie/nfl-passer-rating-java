Welcome to my NFL Passer Rating page

Want to know how well your favorite team's Quarterback is throwing the football? Check out my NFL Passer Rating GUI made entirely by Java. Enter the player, add their stats to the input and a quarterback rating is compiled, from 0 to the perfect 158.3. You can even change the background showcasing your favorite NFL team to make you feel better about your team.

Instructions

To open your program, simply download the file, either in zip or tar.gz form. For those who download it in a zip file, unzip the file and open the NFLPasserRating.jar file in the dist folder. In order to run the program, Java must be installed on your computer.
